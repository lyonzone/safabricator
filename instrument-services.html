Instrumentation Services

We understand that Instrumentation is critical for ensuring stable, accurate and reliable control of process. We offer following Instrumentation services:

  • & of Transmitters, Switches & Gauges for Pressure, Temperature Flow and Level applic, Control Valves, on / off valves, Breather valves, Safety Valves, panels, junction boxes, cable laying & termin, perforated cable tray work, pedestal fabric.
  • & of DCS and PLC systems, SCADA systems, hazardous area control systems, fire and gas systems and process , termin & commissioning of indicators, transmitters and controllers, valves & other field Instrumentations.
  • & including cabling, marshalling panels, Instrumentation air/gas tubing and impulse piping work.
  • Testing, calibr & loop checking through fully trained technicians with certified testing equipment.
  • Specialist knowledge on industrial projects for material selection and procurement related to electrical & Instrumentation.
  • Ability to pre-fabricate Instrumentation support & other fabric work for control packages on or off site.
  • of Manpower like skilled Engineers, Technicians for shut down maintenance, and commissioning activities on deput basis.

Testing

  • In view of the increased needs for the certific and testing of Electrical s, & Instrumentation testing we now offer a comprehensive testing, calibr NABL certific service.

Commissioning

We provide commissioning assistance on all size projects by :

  • Having people with experience in the commissioning of Instrumentation & Electrical control systems in a diverse range of processes within any environment.
  • Provide document that is traceable to internal standards.
